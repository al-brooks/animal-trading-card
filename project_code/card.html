Pikachu

The red circles on a Pikachu's cheeks serve as a storage place of electricity and light up to a near white hue once electricity is released.

  • Occupation: World's Greatest Detective
  • Favorite Food: Donuts with an electric charge
  • Favorite Hobby: Solving the unsolvable
  • Habitat: Places with a spark of mystery

Pikachu is a mouse type Pokemon that stands a little over 1 feet tall. They are able to harness electricity as a defense mechanism. Detective work is a rare occupation for a Pikachu, but don't let that fool you. They have the potential to uncover the most shocking mysteries.
